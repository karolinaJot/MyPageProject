My name is Karolina Jaklik, I make ideas become reality.

I am a computer science engineer with over 2 years of experience in IT, the majority of which is in the commercial sector. I have experience working in both small teams and large international corporations. I communicate in both English and Polish on a daily basis at work.

Currently, since June 2023, I have been working for LTI Mindtree as a Cloud & Infrastructure Engineer, with Microsoft as my client. The technologies I work with daily include Azure Cloud and Azure DevOps.

Between 2022 and 2023, I worked for the Krakow-based startup Swormcheck as a JavaScript programmer. In a small team, I developed a modern web application for time management, project management, and vacation requests. I gathered user needs and feedback, based on which I designed the appearance and architecture of various features. I collaborated closely with both the business and end users. The main technologies I worked with daily were React, TypeScript, Material UI, Azure Cloud, Azure DevOps, Node.js, and MongoDB.

I enjoy implementing solutions from start to finish, using various technologies along the way. I love learning and experimenting.
